About

I'm Lisa, developer and owner of a boutique web development shop. My interests are in getting to know people which is reflected in my art. I enjoy collaborating with clients to help them uncover the story of their business. Increasing a business's online presence will help to connect with current and potential customers. By getting to know you, I will better be able to create a website that is tailored for you and your needs.


Portfolio

Web application created with Kyla Bendt and Lindsay Atencio that uses a map API to display property values and crime incident reports in the City of Albuquerque. The intention behind this project is to allow people to find affordable homes in areas with low crimes.

With this portrait of an African woman, my intention was to portray her realistically and convey the life that she has lived - her past, present and future. "Hope", charcoal on paper, 18" x 24". Portrait of an Awa indigenous woman from eastern Brazilian Amazon and her pet monkey. My desire here is to give a face to those impacted by threats to their land and way of life from industries. "Woman", charcoal on paper, 16" x 20".

This collage uses a mix of photo realism, pop art, and silhouette. It is my attempt to capture how some may be thinking and feeling about the current state of the U.S. "Chaos", acrylic on canvas, 14" x 18".
